Famous BBQ Chicken Wings!

Description

The best thing you can have on a Sunday afternoon is nice serving of bbq wings.

All you need is a pack of chicken wings and some good tasty bbq sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Seasoning
  • Garlic Butter
  • BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Pre heat air fryer to 425F
  2. Seperate Flats & Drumsticks
  3. Dry off Wings and coat in butter
  4. Place wings in air fryer for 20 minutes
  5. Remove and tossed in BBQ
