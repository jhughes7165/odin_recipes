Yummy Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

Description

This is the quickest and yummy snack to have after school or leaving a hard day of coding.

All you need is cheese, bread, butter, and a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Cheese
  • 2 slices Bread
  • Table spoon of Garlic Butter

Steps

  1. Pre heat frying pan to medium heat
  2. place a tlb of butter in pan
  3. Place two slices of bread in the pan and crust the bottom and flip
  4. Place the cheese on each of the toasted sides
  5. put on side of bread on the other (cheese on cheese)
  6. Cook for additonal 3 minutes and remove
