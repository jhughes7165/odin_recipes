Famous Chickpea Crust Pizza Recipe!

Description

Are you ready to taste the best "healthy" pizza ever!? Well, you're in the right place. This pizza is amazing for people who can not stomach traditional flour based dough. It is quick and delicious with your family begging you to make it every night. You can add any types of meat on top with the best provolone cheese you can find.

The key to making any pizza is having the correct equipment such as a pizza stone, pizza handler, and a prep table for cutting and rolling dough. Pre heat the oven to 425F with the pizza stone for at least 1 hours. The point is the create an even heat distrubtion across the pizza stone that will cook the crust at an even rate. Add your desired pizza sauce, toppings and alot of cheese! Place pizza in oven and cook for 10 minutes. Done!! Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Chickpea Crust
  • 1 oz Pizza Sauce
  • 1 table spponGarlic Butter
  • Chopped up Pepperoni 1oz
  • 5 oz Mozzarella
  • Chopped up Ham 1oz
  • Chooped up Grounded Sausage 1oz

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 450F with stone for 1 hour
  2. Remove Frozen ChickPea Crust from Frezzer
  3. Spread Garlic Butter over curst(expecially the edges)
  4. Spread pizza sauce over crust
  5. Add meat and cheese toppings
  6. Place in oven on stone for 10 minutes
